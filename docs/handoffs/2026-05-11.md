## Decisions made and why

### 1. No-scraping policy (SPEC §2)

User course-correction: *"do not source from sources that require scraping, use only those with RSS or API."* Codified as a hard out in §2. Twitter/X via nitter, RSS-Bridge, RSSHub-with-cookies, etc. are excluded by policy.

### 2. Cloudflare Email Worker bridge (SPEC §6.9 — new)

Initial exploration considered **RSSHub vs RSS-Bridge** for Twitter — abandoned per the no-scraping policy. Then considered **Kill The Newsletter (self-host) vs Cloudflare Email Worker** for the inevitable email-only sources (Anthropic, Meta AI, HF Daily Papers, etc.). User picked CF Worker after asking for the comparison.

Why CF Worker over KTN:
- VPS-independent — newsletters keep arriving during VPS reboots / Odoo redeploys.
- CF Email Routing has built-in anti-spam; KTN-on-VPS requires Postfix.
- Optional CF Workers AI pre-processing at intake (free tier).
- Newsletters carry no privacy expectation → "CF sees email contents" doesn't bite.
- User already has CF in their stack (per Railway+CF SSL setup).

Architecture: `socialisn.com` (CF-managed) → catch-all Email Routing → Email Worker (postal-mime parse, strip boilerplate) → D1 → `feed-handler` Worker serves Atom at `https://inbox.socialisn.com/feeds/<slug>.xml`. Addresses follow `<slug>@socialisn.com` pattern; the Worker uses the To: local-part as the source slug (rather than parsing From: header).

7 seed bridge sources: Anthropic news, Meta AI blog, HF Daily Papers, Brad Setser (Follow the Money), Heatmap News, Robinson Meyer (Shift Key), Derek Lowe (In the Pipeline).
